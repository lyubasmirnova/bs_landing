We're your one-stop-shop for all your crushing, sorting, and transportation equipment needs.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to customer satisfaction, and are always open to suggestions and ideas to help improve our services and solutions. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you achieve your goals.

  • We are your trusted partner.
  • Our team of highly skilled professionals.
  • Our extensive warehouse features a wide selection of spare parts.

We offer a comprehensive range of services and solutions, leveraging our expertise in design, manufacturing, and delivery of equipment for crushing, sorting, and transportation of bulk cargoes. Our focus is on delivering efficient, reliable, and innovative solutions that help our clients achieve their goals and succeed in their industry. Our team of highly skilled professionals has the education and experience to tackle any problem or desire you may have. With a vast inventory of spare parts for Russian and European equipment and a wide selection of second-hand machinery, including crushers, screens, and conveyors, we have everything you need to keep your operation running smoothly. And with our commitment to customer satisfaction and willingness to consider equipment purchase proposals, you can count on us to be your trusted partner. Let us help you take your business to the next level.
